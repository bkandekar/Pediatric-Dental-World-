Dr. Sunny Smile, DDS

Chief Pediatric Dentist
BOARD CERTIFIED PEDIATRICIAN

Dr. Sunny Smile, DDS

"My primary goal is to guide children to understand that dental care is safe, playful, and rewarding. A positive dental visit during childhood shapes their attitude toward healthcare for the rest of their lives."

Dr. Sunny Smile completed his Doctorate of Dental Surgery (DDS) from the California Dental University, graduating with honors. He immediately pursued three additional years of residency specializing in Pediatric Clinical Care at Children's Hospital CA.

He is a standing executive member of the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry (AAPD) and specializes in treating severe dental anxiety, child behavior management, and pediatric sedation protocols.

Education & Training

DDS: California Dental University (Honors Graduate)

Residency: Specialized Pediatric Dental Residency, Children's Hospital CA

Board Certification: American Board of Pediatric Dentistry Diplomate

Dr. Joy Giggles, DDS

Orthodontist & Kids Specialist
PEDIATRIC ORTHODONTIST

Dr. Joy Giggles, DDS

"Early jaw alignment monitoring and interceptive orthopedic dental plates prevent extreme orthodontic extraction needs as teenagers. A straight smile is a happy, confident smile."

Dr. Joy Giggles holds a specialization in Interceptive Pediatric Orthodontics. She has dedicated her career to understanding jaw development dynamics in children ages 4 to 12.

Known for her playful, gentle laughter and interactive magic tricks, she has a natural talent for making dental impressions and brace fittings an exciting, fully cooperative game for toddlers and children.

Education & Training

DDS: National Orthodontic Institute (Summa Cum Laude)

Master's Degree: Master of Science in Pediatric Craniofacial Growth

Board Certification: Board Certified Orthodontic Diplomate
