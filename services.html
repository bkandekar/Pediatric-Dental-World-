PREVENTIVE CARE

Gentle Cleaning & Oral Examinations

A key cornerstone of our pediatric approach is protecting your child's natural teeth from cavities and decay. Our specialists use professional child-sized tools to gently clean and remove plaque, followed by a playful lesson in correct daily brushing.

  • Gentle hand-scaling
  • Plaque detection
  • Playful dental education
  • Infant dental care

RESTORATIVE DENTISTRY

Painless Cavity Treatments & Fillings

Should a cavity form, there's no reason to worry. We specialize in entirely painless restorative dentistry. By applying numbing jellies first, we can fill cavities using aesthetic, metal-free, white composite resin bonding that matches natural enamel perfectly.

  • Strong composite resins
  • Safe sedation options
  • Kid-friendly root canals
  • Gentle extractions

Clinical Safety

Our Hospital-Grade Safety Standards

Keeping kids healthy is our absolute priority. Here is how we ensure maximum clinical sanitation.

Class-B Autoclaves

We use premium vacuum autoclaves to sterilize clinical handpieces and instruments, killing 100% of bacterial and viral spores.

Single-Use Barriers

Where reusable sterilization is not applicable, clinical surfaces are wrapped in medical-grade plastic barriers changed between patients.

HEPA Clean-Air Filters

Every treatment room is outfitted with continuous HEPA and UV air filtration, cycling and cleaning the indoor air every 8 minutes.
